About the Guide

Welcome to The Finer Years - IndyStar's Guide to Retirement Living and Elder Care. This comprehensive, local resource will help you learn about the levels of retirement living and elder care from low maintenance/maintenance-free to specialized 24-hour nursing care.

Living and Care Types

Compare options in the area to fit the needs you have for yourself or aging loved ones. To learn more about the options, please go to the Living & Care link at the upper right-hand navigation bar and select Types of Living and Care for a brief description of each level of living and care.

Search Listings

There are multiple ways you can search communities and providers in your area. On the home page, you will find a search box where you can search by location and/or living & care type. The dropdowns will guide selections for your search.

When you click the Search button, communities and providers will appear within a grid with basic information. By selecting the map view, you can see these listings on a map of the area.

Featured Communities

Listings at the top of your search results are marked as "Featured" communities and providers. From these listings, you'll be able to find a link to their web site, send an email to the property for more information and access a deeper profile which will enable you to learn more about that community or provider.

The community profile also includes a photo gallery, list of amenities, and more information about the living and care types that are provided.

Saved Listings

You can save the communities or providers you are interested in by clicking on the star icon beside the community information. You will be asked to login to ensure you can access saved listings during subsequent visits during your consideration journey.

Consideration of your own options or those of a loved one for retirement living and elder care is an important and often challenging process. We look forward to providing a resource to your research and invite you to check back frequently for new information and listings to help guide you through this process. Communities and Providers presented in this guide do not represent an endorsement of their services by IndyStar.

If you have a questions or suggestions as to how to make this guide more useful please email us. We thank you for using this resource and know it will help you in your consideration.
